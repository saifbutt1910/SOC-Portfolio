Week 1 - Networking Basics

What is an IP Address?
An IP address is a unique address assigned to a device on a network. It helps devices identify and communicate with each other over the internet or local network.

Example:
192.168.1.1

What Does TCP/IP Mean?
TCP/IP stands for Transmission Control Protocol / Internet Protocol.

- IP is responsible for delivering data to the correct device.
- TCP ensures the data is delivered correctly and completely.

Together, TCP/IP allows devices to communicate reliably over networks and the internet.
