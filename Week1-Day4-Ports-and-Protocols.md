Week 1 Day 4 - Ports and Protocols

What are Ports?
Ports are communication endpoints used by services and applications on a computer.

Common Ports
- Port 80 = HTTP
- Port 443 = HTTPS
- Port 22 = SSH
- Port 21 = FTP

Why Ports are Important
Ports help computers know which service or application should receive network traffic.

What I Learned Today
- IP addresses identify devices.
- Ports identify services running on devices.
- Cybersecurity professionals monitor ports to detect suspicious activity.
